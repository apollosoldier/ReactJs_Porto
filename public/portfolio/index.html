About Me

Hello, my name is Traoré M Aka "ApolloSoldier". I am a student in computer science and electronics at ESIREM Dijon and my first goal is to become an embedded system engineer. I like nature, travel, sport, cinema and literature. My favorite activities are mountain trekking, bungee jumping, box thai and UAV drone manufacturing. When my dev senses kick-in I build presumably awesome stuff. I stay close to the community and try to keep tabs with the pace at which the web is evolving. I also like to blog what I learn and share my travels pictures. I built this site from scratch. By scratch, I mean absolutely from scratch without any UI library/framework (except React though) and had so much fun along the way. React-Redux, Node.js, Ruby on Rails, Java, Spring Boot, Docker and AWS are the main tricks up my sleeve. I am also obsessed with making the web look pretty with SASS/CSS. \ (•◡•) /
